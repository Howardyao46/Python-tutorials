Lists (eg. lenny, asheng)

Meet Lenny and Asheng!

Today, we a going to make a list

-----------------------------------------

Type in this code as your first line:

---------------

developers = ['lenny', 'asheng']

---------------

This makes a list called developers, consisting of two poeple: Lenny and Asheng.

Now let us add some stuff to the list

---------------

developers.append('notADeveloper')

developers.append('alsoNotADeveloper')

---------------

Let's print our list!

---------------

print(developers[0])

print(developers[1])

print(developers[2])

print(developers[3])

OUTPUT:

lenny

asheng

notADeveloper

alsoNotADeveloper
