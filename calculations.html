Calculations with Python

This will teach you to do calculations in Python.

Open the Python shell and just type: 5 + 9

You will get an output of 14.

As you know, + and - means addition and subtraction. But you might not know that * and / mean multiplication and division. + = addition, - = subtraction, * = multiplication, / = division

Now type 5 * 8. You should get 40.

Of course, you can also type print(5 * 8)

And you'll, again, get 40 as an output.

Lesson complete!
